3) What data may be retained

We may retain certain records only where required by law or for legitimate purposes such as fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or compliance (for example, transaction records). When retained, we protect the data and delete it when the retention period ends.

4) Processing time

  • We typically process deletion requests within 7–30 days.
  • If additional verification is needed, we will contact you via email.

5) Deleting local data (on your device)

If your data is stored locally on your device, you can remove it by using the in-app “Reset data” (if available) or uninstalling the app.
