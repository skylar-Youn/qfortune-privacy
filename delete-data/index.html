1) Delete data directly in the app (Recommended)

In-App Delete

You can delete some or all of your data without deleting your account.

  • On the relevant data/profile screen, tap the Delete button located next to the “Edit” button.
  • Once you confirm, the selected data (e.g., profile/inputs) will be removed from the app.

2) Data types that can be deleted

Examples of data that may be deleted include:

  • Fortune/personality input data: name (or nickname), gender, date of birth, calendar type (solar/lunar), place of birth, and (optional) birth time
  • Generated profiles/analysis history stored in the app (where applicable)

3) If you need help (Email request)

If you cannot delete data in the app, you may request data deletion by email:

Email: skyutech@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your platform (Android/iOS) and app version (optional)
  • What you want deleted: partial data or all data
  • Your in-app nickname/identifier (if applicable)

4) Processing time

  • In-app deletions take effect immediately.
  • Email requests are typically processed within 7–30 days.
